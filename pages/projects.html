Vegii

Vegii is your pocket guide to identifying vegan-friendly products in Japan. Designed for non-Japanese speakers, Vegii makes it easy to check whether an item is vegan — simply scan the barcode or ingredient list, and get instant results in English.

MonDaiNai is a mobile application that connects individuals who wish to give away items with those in need. The platform streamlines the entire process of posting items to arranging meetups and facilitating communication across language barriers.

ProjectSenpai

ProjectSenpai is a web application that leverages an AI-powered chatbot to help users generate and refine ideas for web development projects. Through interactive prompts, the chatbot suggests features, technologies, and project directions—helping developers turn initial concepts into actionable plans.
